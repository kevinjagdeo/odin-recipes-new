Baked Chicken Wings

Description

This is a very simple baked chicken wings recipe that won't take up too much of your time and requires no skill! All you need is a few simple ingredients and access to an oven. Let's begin!


Ingredients

As mentioned, you only need a few simple ingredients. Here is what you need:

  • 1.5lbs of chicken wings
  • 2tbsp Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper


Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees.
  2. Pat dry all of the chicken wings with paper towel and put in a bowl. Make sure they are as dry as possible: this will be key in getting them super crispy!
  3. Mix the wings with the baking powder, making sure to get even coverage.
  4. Evenly distribute the wings on a foil-lined tray. It helps to use parchment paper in addition to foil.
  5. Bake for approximately 30-40 minutes, flipping halfway.
  6. While the wings are still hot, toss with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Serve with your favourite sauce and enjoy!
